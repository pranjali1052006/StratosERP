📊 1. Academic Oversight & Analytics

The Class Incharge serves as the primary monitor for the collective progress and success of an entire division.  

    Class Analytics: Access comprehensive performance and attendance analytics for the assigned class.  

    Comprehensive Marks View: Monitor all internal, external, and supplementary marks across every subject taken by students in the division.  

    Early Alert System: Utilize an AI-driven dashboard that flags "at-risk" students by cross-referencing low attendance (below 75%), dipping GPA trends, and unaddressed grievances.  

    AICTE Activity Tracking: Utilize a dedicated tracker to monitor total points earned by each student, ensuring they meet the graduation requirements set by the Admin.  

💬 2. Communication & Coordination

The Class Incharge acts as the central link between the department, students, and parents.  

    Digital PTM Portfolio: Generate pre-compiled PDFs for each student summarizing attendance, subject-wise marks, AICTE points, and active grievances for parent-teacher meetings.  

    Parent/TG Hub: Maintain direct contact info for parents and Teacher Guardians to facilitate proactive student support.  

    Tiered Notice Board: Broadcast department-specific opportunities, hackathons, and urgent warnings to the assigned class.  

    Resource Management: Monitor study materials and class notes uploaded via MinIO to ensure students have necessary academic resources.  

🔄 3. Triage & Lifecycle Management

The system utilizes AI to route specific administrative and interpersonal issues to the Class Incharge.  

    Interpersonal Conflict Resolution: Serve as the resolution authority for grievances related to peer conflicts, as routed by Gemini AI.  

    Attendance Intervention: Receive and act upon AI-flagged notices for students falling below institutional thresholds.  

    Backlog Monitoring: Follow up with students carrying [KT] or [SUPPLI] badges to ensure they remain on the path to graduation.  

    Progression Oversight: Review student readiness for transition to the next academic year or the Alumni section.  

🔑 4. Operational Context

The role is defined by a strict administrative scope to ensure data integrity and institutional standards.  

    Multi-Designation Support: Faculty can simultaneously hold the Class Incharge role alongside Subject Incharge and Teacher Guardian roles.  

    HOD Assignment: Roles and data access rights are assigned by the Head of Department for the duration of the active semester.  

    Unified Identity: All student tracking is governed by the institutional Email ID and standardized UID string protocol.
