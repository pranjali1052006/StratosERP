📊 1. Departmental Analytics & Oversight

The HOD monitors the overall health and performance of their specific branch through high-level data visualization.  

    Aggregated Analytics: Access branch-wide performance metrics, attendance trends, placement statistics, and exam results.  

    Student Tracking: Monitor individual student dashboards, including [KT] and [SUPPLI] badges, to track academic progression within the department.  

    Alumni Data Access: View graduation years, final CGPAs, and placement data for students who have transitioned to the Alumni section.  

    Syllabus Pacing Monitoring: (Optional) Review real-time semester pacing reports generated by AI comparing faculty lecture logs against the master syllabus.  

💬 2. Faculty Management & Coordination

The HOD is responsible for the operational deployment of faculty resources within their branch.  

    Subject Allocation: Assign specific subjects and classes to faculty members for the active semester.  

    RBAC Designation: Assign faculty to specific roles, such as Class Incharge, Subject Incharge, or Teacher Guardian.  

    Notice Broadcasting: Draft and distribute formal institutional notices specifically to their assigned branch.  

    Study Material Oversight: Monitor the distribution of notes and PDFs uploaded by faculty to the MinIO object storage.  

🔄 3. Triage & Administrative Action

The HOD serves as an escalation point for technical and administrative issues.  

    Grievance Escalation: Act as the secondary authority for academic marks disputes if they remain unresolved by the Subject Incharge.  

    AI-Powered Notice Generation: Utilize Gemini AI to draft formal communications, such as low-attendance warnings, for departmental distribution.  

    Leave Management Oversight: Monitor the scheduling engine's deterministic timetable generation and the assignment of substitute teachers for planned or emergency leaves.  

🔑 4. Operational Context

The HOD operates within a defined scope that bridges the gap between the System Admin and the Faculty.  

    Branch-Specific Scope: Access and action rights are strictly restricted to the department they lead.  

    Multi-Role Capability: While serving as HOD, they may also hold faculty-level designations for specific subjects.  

    Semester Configuration Alignment: Departmental actions are governed by the global semester dates and active semester type (ODD/EVEN) set by the Admin.
