📊 1. Mentorship & Student Performance

The Teacher Guardian (TG) acts as a personal advisor and mentor, focusing on the individual growth and holistic development of a designated student group.  

    Student Allocation: Manages a focused cohort of approximately 20 students for the duration of the semester.  

    AICTE Point Management: Evaluates and awards AICTE points to students for extracurricular and co-curricular achievements.  

    Teacher Guardian Assistant: Utilizes Gemini AI to process mentee grades and attendance to auto-generate personalized "Areas of Improvement" reports.  

    Performance Monitoring: Accesses mentee dashboards to track marks, attendance trends, and unresolved [KT] or [SUPPLI] badges.  

💬 2. Communication & Grievance Resolution

The TG serves as the primary link between the institution, the student, and the parent for personal and interpersonal matters.  

    Direct Parent Communication: Facilitates formal communication with parents regarding student progress, behavioral concerns, or excellence.  

    Interpersonal Grievance Triage: Acts as the designated resolution authority for AI-routed tickets regarding "Interpersonal/peer conflict".  

    PTM Leadership: Uses AI-generated insights and student portfolios to lead meaningful discussions during Parent-Teacher Meetings (PTMs).  

    Notice Review: Monitors institutional broadcasts to ensure mentees are aware of relevant hackathons, scholarship opportunities, or urgent warnings.  

🔄 3. Operational Context & Integration

The TG role is fully integrated into the StratosERP ecosystem to ensure seamless support through the student lifecycle.  

    Multi-Designation Support: Faculty can simultaneously serve as a TG while holding Subject Incharge or Class Incharge roles.  

    Dashboard Synchronization: All points awarded and evaluations made by the TG are reflected instantly on the Student Academic Dashboard.  

    Automated Routing: Gemini AI intelligently identifies the TG as the correct authority for specific student-centric issues.  

🔑 4. Administrative Scope

The TG operates within specific jurisdictional limits to maintain institutional standards and data privacy.  

    HOD Assignment: Mentees are assigned to specific Teacher Guardians by the Head of Department for the active semester.  

    Data Privacy: Access rights are strictly restricted to the ~20 assigned students within their mentorship group.  

    Lifecycle Support: Collaborates with the Class Incharge to ensure mentees are academically and administratively ready for batch progression.
